There will be two rounds. Before the first round there is a practice round that will not be paid. The second round will be recorded and potentially paid as a bonus payment.

In each round there are two stages:

  • Stage 1: You begin collecting boxes by pressing ’Start’ and stop the collection process by hitting ’Stop’. Boxes are collected randomly. Once collected, boxes are marked with a check-mark.
  • Stage 2: You submit your collected boxes and finish the round. To submit your collected boxes and move to the next round, hit ’Next’.

Your results will be recorded and provided to you only once you have completed both sections of the experiment.

Types of Boxes

There are two types of boxes: bomb-boxes and point-boxes.

  • There is one bomb-box hidden amongst the 100 boxes. All other boxes are point-boxes. Points are converted to payoffs at the rate of 1 point =$0.01.
  • Each point-box you collect gives you 1 point. These points are potential and depend on whether you collect a bomb-box.
  • If there is a bomb: If you collect the bomb-box, all your earnings for that round are destroyed and you receive nothing.
  • You do not know where the bomb is. You only know that the bomb can be in any place with equal likelihood. You will not know if you collected a bomb in any round until the end of the experiment.
  • Each round is independent. If you collect a bomb in one round, it has no eﬀect on the earnings from previous or subsequent rounds.

We now begin the game with a trial-round. The results from this trial-round will not be recorded, nor will they be eligible for the potential bonus payment.
